furina

Before, when she was a single being, Foçalors, the Goddess of Justice, separated her divinity from her body and spirit, leaving behind her human self, Furina. Furina was cursed with immortality as long as her divine self continued to exist, and she had to oversee Fontaine as the interim Hydro Archon for 500 years to carry out the true Archon’s plan to deceive the Celestial Order and save Fontaine from a great prophecy. After the execution of Foçalors and the destruction of the Divine Throne of the Hydro Archon, Furina left her role and now lives her life as an ordinary human.

Nahida

She is the vessel of the Lesser Lord Kusanali, the current Dendro Archon. Having been freed from her long confinement in the Surasthana Sanctuary, she now strives to have a stronger presence in Sumeru.

Shogun Raiden

She controls a puppet while meditating within the Plane of Euthymia. She is the vessel of Beelzebul, the current Electro Archon of Inazuma. She made her first appearance as an NPC in Version 2.0 and became playable for the first time in Version 2.1 in the "Reign of Serenity" banner.

Zhongli

He is the consultant for the El Camino Funeral Home. He is later revealed to be Morax's current vessel, the Archon Geo, who has decided to experience the world from a mortal's perspective. After renouncing his Gnosis, he retires from his position as Archon and returns to his former identity as Adeptus, although still under the guise of a human. He made his first appearance as an NPC in Version 1.0 and first arrived in Version 1.1 in the gachapon "Solitude of the Nobility".

Venti

A bard that seems to have arrived on some unknown wind — sometimes sings songs as old as the hills, and other times sings poems fresh and new. Likes apples and lively places but is not a fan of cheese or anything sticky. When using his Anemo power to control the wind, it often appears as feathers, as he's fond of that which appears light and breezy.

Mavuika

Haborym, also known as Mavuika and by her ancient name "Kiongozi", is the Goddess of War and the current Pyro Archon presiding over Natlan. He is a non-playable character and member of The Seven. She makes her first appearance during Genshin Impact Preview – Ignition: A Name Forged in Flames.
