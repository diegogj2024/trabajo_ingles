Mondstadt

This is the nation of freedom. Ruled by Venti, the Anemo Archon. Despite being the God of freedom since that is how he likes to feel, which is why he left his nation total freedom, this is nothing more than a wind spirit "trapped" in the body of his sub best friend.

Liyue

This is the Nation of contracts. It was ruled by the Geo Archon, Zhongli. Currently he retired from the God position to live a quiet and peaceful life as a mortal. Considered the strongest Archon/God of all.

Inazuma

This is the nation of the eternity. Ruled by the Archon and God, Shogun Raiden. Whose name is from a puppet created by the Electro Archon based on herself to be in command of the Nation. The Electro Archon's real name is Raiden Ei.

Sumeru

This is the nation of wisdom. Her Archon is Nahida, the Lesser Queen Kusanali who had been imprisoned for 500 years by her own nation, in order to create another God. Thanks to the help of the traveler and his friends they were able to free her and thus, she took control of her nation, being respected and loved by her people.

Fontaine

This is the nation of Justice. Her previous "Archon" was Furina, who played the role of Goddess to save her nation from a catastrophic prophecy. The true hydro Archon, Focalors, divided her human part from her divinity, thus creating Furina to play the Goddess of justice for 500 years.

Natlan

The nation of war. Ruled by the Pyro Archon and Goddess of War, Mavuika. This nation is in a constant war with the abyss. The abyss is a "void" where there is only chaos, monsters and destruction. That is why the Archon and her people fight with all their might to protect their nation from disaster.
