Genshin Impact
is an action role-playing video game developed by miHoYo,
published by miHoYo in mainland China and worldwide by HoYoverse for the Android, iOS, Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, and PlayStation 5 platforms.
It is a free-to-play title monetized through a gacha system,
which receives frequent updates using the game-as-a-service model.
It was released on September 28, 2020. An Xbox Series X/S version is set to release on November 20, 2024.
A Nintendo Switch version is also set to release, though a release window has not been announced.


Development began in 2017 and it draws inspiration from a variety of sources,
including The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, Gnosticism, and mythologies. Since its release, Genshin Impact has been positively received by critics, being praised for its combat mechanics and immersive open world. The game is considered a commercial success, grossing over $3.7 billion during its first year, making it the highest-grossing title per launch in the video game industry.

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game that allows the player to control one of four interchangeable characters in a party. The player can control their character and perform actions such as running, climbing, swimming, and sliding, all limited by stamina. Some characters possess abilities that can alter the environment, such as freezing water to create an ice path that can help the player traverse terrain. The map features teleportation points that allow the player to move quickly.
